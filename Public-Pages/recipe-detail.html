Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Instructions

  1. 1

    Prep the Chicken

    Slice the chicken breasts horizontally to create thinner cutlets. Season generously with garlic powder, salt, and black pepper on both sides.

  2. 2

    Sear the Meat

    In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook for 5 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.

  3. 3

    Make the Sauce

    In the same pan, add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Reduce heat to medium-low and pour in the heavy cream. Simmer for 3 minutes until slightly thickened.

  4. 4

    Combine & Serve

    Stir in the parmesan cheese and spinach. Cook until spinach wilts. Return the chicken to the pan and spoon the sauce over it. Serve hot over pasta or rice.
