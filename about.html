About NutriChef

Empowering your culinary journey with smart nutrition insights.

Who We Are

More Than Just Recipes.

NutriChef is a modern web platform designed for food lovers, home cooks, and health-conscious individuals.

We combine the joy of sharing recipes with the power of data-driven nutrition. Our goal is to make cooking easier, healthier, and more informative for everyone.

The Challenge

The "Nutrition Gap"

Many people cook without knowing the nutritional value of their meals, leading to unbalanced diets. Most platforms focus only on taste, ignoring health.

NutriChef closes this gap. We provide automatic calorie and macro calculations for every recipe you share or discover.

Our Mission & Scope

Built with purpose. Designed for impact.

Primary Goals

  • Upload & share custom recipes.
  • Auto-calculate nutrition facts.
  • Track daily calorie intake.
  • Foster a supportive community.

Secondary Goals

  • Responsive & Accessible UI.
  • Interactive visual analytics.
  • Advanced search filtering.
  • Favorites & Collections.

Project Scope

  • In Scope: User Auth, Recipe CRUD, Nutrition Module, Search, User Dashboard.
  • Out of Scope: Mobile App, AI Predictions, Payment Gateway.
