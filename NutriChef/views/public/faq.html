Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about NutriChef.

General

NutriChef is a recipe-sharing and nutrition-tracking platform that helps users discover recipes, upload their own, and view calorie and nutrient information for every meal.

Yes! NutriChef is completely free as a university Web Engineering project.

You can view recipes without an account. However, you need an account to add recipes, rate, comment, save favorites, and use the nutrition tracker.

Recipes & Cooking

Simply go to "Add Recipe" on the navigation menu, fill out the form, upload images, and submit.

Yes, users can manage their own recipes directly from their User Dashboard.

The system uses a predefined nutrition database to estimate calories, proteins, fats, and carbs based on the ingredients you list.

Nutrition & Tracking

A simple tool that helps you track your daily calorie intake based on the recipes you eat.

Yes — the tracker lets you add custom meals and calories manually if the recipe isn't in our database.

Nutrition values are approximate and based on common food databases. They should be used as guidelines, not strictly for medical advice.

Account & Security

Go to the Login/Register page via the top menu and fill in the simple registration form.

This is a student project, but in a real-world scenario, sensitive data like passwords would be stored securely using hashing algorithms.

Yes — in the full version. For this project demo, password reset functionality may be simulated.
